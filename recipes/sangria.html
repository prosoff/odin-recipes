Ron's Sangria!

Try some of Ron's Infamous Sangria!

This recipe was found amongst a collection of old match boxes my father had collected. This recipe is perfectly Ron - not consistent measurements and a ton of booze!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Sugar
  • 2 ? Cointreau
  • 2 ? Brandy
  • 2 Shots Lemonade
  • 2 Shots Banana Liquor
  • 2/3 Liter of Rose
  • Bananas
  • Oranges
  • Lemons

Steps

  1. Mix all liquid in large pitcher
  2. Add fruit and stir
  3. Chill and serve in wine glasses
